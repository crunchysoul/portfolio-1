About Me

As a young kid, I have always been fascinated with computers but never knew that I would fall in love with building software. Thus in the university, I took the opportunity to take Computer Science as my Bachelor degree. After graduation, I started working as an IT Consultant in the Philippines where I was able to practice some web development. I eventually grew fond of it and have been learning different frameworks ever since.

In 2017, I decided to move to Melbourne to start a new life and further my career as a software developer by attending the Coder Academy Bootcamp. I discovered that this boot camp journey would lead me to better software designs and software development disciplines. I consider my career choice as my greatest success as this is the path that I have always wanted to pursue.

SMART Goals Terminal App

A ruby application that will let people write down their goals using the SMART criteria (i.e. Smart, Realistic, Attainable, Relevant and Timely). We believe that the best way for people to achieve their goals is to write about them and do little bits of tasks which lead to them attain their objectives.

The app features a scheduler which lets users set the frequency of their tasks and notifies them by email or through desktop notifications.
