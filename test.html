About Me

As a young kid, I have always been fascinated with computers but never knew that I would fall in love with building software. Thus in the university, I took the opportunity to take Computer Science as my Bachelor degree. After graduating, I started working as an IT Consultant in the Philippines in which I was able to practice some web development and grew fond of it. In 2017, I decided to move to Melbourne and further my career as a software developer by attending the Coder Academy Bootcamp course. I discovered that the journey ahead would lead me to better designs and better software development practices. I consider my career choice as my greatest success. It is the path that I have always wanted.

SMART Goals Terminal App

A ruby application that will let people write down their goals using the SMART criteria (i.e. Smart, Realistic, Attainable, Relevant and Timely). We believe that the best way for people to achieve their goals is to write about them and do little bits of tasks which lead to them attain their objectives.

The app features a scheduler which lets users set the frequency of their tasks and notifies them by email or through desktop notifications.
